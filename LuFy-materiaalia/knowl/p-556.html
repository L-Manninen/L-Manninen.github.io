Lasketaan voimien tekemät työt pistetulolla \(W=\vec{F}\cdot\Delta\vec{r}\text{.}\) Siirtymä on \(\Delta \vec{r}=-5,00\hat{j}\text{.}\) Painovoimalle

\begin{equation*} w = \vec{F}_\mathrm{G} \cdot \Delta \vec{r} = (F_\mathrm{G})(\Delta r)\cos\ang{0} = (\SI{255}{\kilogram})(\SI{9,81}{\meter/\second\squared})(\SI{5,00}{\meter})(1,00) = \SI{1,25\eplus 4}{\joule} \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} w = \vec{T}_1 \cdot \Delta \vec{r} = (T_1)(\Delta r)\cos\ang{150} = (\SI{1830}{\newton})(\SI{5,00}{\meter})(-0,8660) = \SI{-7,92\eplus 3}{\joule} \end{equation*}

Jännitysvoimalle 2
