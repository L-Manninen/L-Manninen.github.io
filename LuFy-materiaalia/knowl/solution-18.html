Ratkaisu 1.7.2.1.3.1.

Integroidaan järjestyksessä

\begin{align*} x_{\text{MKP}} \amp= \int_{y=0}^{y=y_1} \int_{x=0}^{x=x_1} \int_{z=0}^{z=g(x)} x\rho dzdxdy \\ \amp= \rho \int_{0}^{y_1} \int_{0}^{x_1} \frac{z_1 (x_1-x)}{x_1} x dxdy \\ \amp= \rho \int_{0}^{y_1} \frac{z_1}{x_1} \int_{0}^{x_1} (x_1 x - x^2) dxdy \\ \amp= \rho \int_{0}^{y_1} \frac{z_1}{x_1} \Big|_{0}^{x_1} \left(\frac{1}{2}x_1 x^2 - \frac{1}{3} x^3 \right) dy \\ \amp= \rho \int_{0}^{y_1} \frac{z_1}{x_1} \left(\frac{1}{2}x_1^3 - \frac{1}{3} x_1^3 \right) dy \\ \amp= \rho \frac{1}{6} z_1 x_1^2 \Big|_{0}^{y_1} y \\ \amp= \frac{1}{6} \rho x_1^2 y_1 z_1 \\ \amp= 0,786\text{m} \end{align*}
